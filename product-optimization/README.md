# Gumroad Product Optimization — Deliverables

Strategic assets for relaunching the **AI Automation Assistant** ($280) Gumroad
product with a YouTube → email → product funnel.

## Contents

| Folder | Purpose | Use when |
|---|---|---|
| `1-lead-magnet/` | Free PDF + GitHub repo structure | Top-of-funnel trust artifact. Linked from every video and post. |
| `2-video-script/` | First YouTube video, end-to-end | Drives traffic to the lead magnet. |
| `3-email-sequence/` | 3-email welcome + sale sequence | Converts magnet downloaders into $280 buyers. |
| `4-gumroad-page/` | Rewritten product page copy | Where the email sequence sends people to buy. |

## The funnel

```
YouTube video (script in 2-video-script/)
   ↓ CTA: "Free PDF in description"
Lead magnet landing page (1-lead-magnet/)
   ↓ Email captured
Email sequence (3-email-sequence/)
   ↓ Day 3 CTA
Gumroad product page (4-gumroad-page/)
   ↓ Purchase
$280 sale → onboarding email → request testimonial
```

## Order of operations

1. Publish lead magnet (PDF + GitHub repo)
2. Update Gumroad page with new copy
3. Wire up email sequence (ConvertKit or Beehiiv)
4. Record + ship video 1
5. Track: video views → PDF downloads → email opens → product page visits → sales

Single conversion benchmark to hit before scaling: **1 sale per 100 PDF downloads.**
